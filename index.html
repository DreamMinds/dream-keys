Each of the fifty-two images in this collection was developed between 1965 and 1968 as part of a stimulus programme investigating symbolic influence on subconscious pattern recognition during sleep. The descriptions below constitute the complete stimulus archive, presented in their original paired form. For further background, see the Field Notes or Protocol documentation.
